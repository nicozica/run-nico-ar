Double Session Z2
Two morning sessions with active rest
Total duration
50 min - 1 hour
Target zone
Z2 (110-129 bpm)
Total distance
7-8 km

Session 1 - Early Morning (7:00-7:30)

20 min Z2 run: 6:45-7:15/km pace (110-129 bpm)
5-10 min cool-down: Easy walk to finish session
Distance estimate: 2.7-3 km total

Rest Between Sessions (30 min - 1 hour)

Active rest: Mate, light fruit/yogurt, move around calmly

Session 2 - Mid Morning (8:30-9:00)

30 min Z2 run: 6:30-7:00/km pace (110-129 bpm)
5-10 min cool-down: Easy walk to finish
Distance estimate: 4.2-4.6 km total

Session 1 Tips

  • Wake up gently: If feeling stiff, start with 5 min walking or very easy jogging
  • Light clothing: You'll warm up quickly
  • Pre-workout fuel: Optional - if needed, just a banana + sip of water
  • Objective: Activate body, increase blood flow, loosen calves and legs

Session 2 Tips

  • Focus on fluidity: Don't seek efficiency, seek looseness
  • Stay hydrated: Even in cool weather
  • Optional finish: Add 2-3 x 50m progressions if legs feel too "loose" at end
  • Objective: Maintain aerobic load without structural fatigue, like split long run

Daily Goal

  • Perceived effort: Low - finish with feeling of lightness, not fatigue
  • Training effect: Aerobic capacity without stress on structure
  • Recovery focus: Should feel energized, not depleted
