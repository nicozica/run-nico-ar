Fartlek Rhythm Play - July 31

Fartlek Session
Rhythm play with variety
Total duration
45 minutes
Format
Variable rhythm play
Purpose
Cardiovascular response

Training Objectives

Cardiovascular response improvement
Rhythm transitions
Running economy & effort perception

Session Structure

Warm-up (10-12 min)
5-6 min easy: >6:40/km (Z2) Gentle activation
3-4 progressions: 20-30s at 5:30-6:00/km + 1 min easy between each
Main Block (25-30 min)
2 min strong: 5:10-5:30/km (RPE 7-8) Controlled, not maximal
2 min easy: 6:40-7:00/km (RPE 3-4) Active recovery
Alternative variations:
  • 3' fast / 1' jog
  • 1' fast / 1' easy x10
  • Pyramid: 1-2-3-2-1 min fast with equal rest
Cool-down (5-8 min)
Conversational pace: Nasal breathing if possible End relaxed

Practical Tips

  • Garmin setup: Set 1-min lap alerts for mental guidance
  • Use terrain: On long hills (like Entre Ríos), use incline for hard segments
  • Wind strategy: Use headwind sections as high-effort intervals
  • Effort over pace: Controlled breathing but agitated in fast sections
  • No leg burn: Should finish sweaty but with good energy
  • Surface choice: Varied surfaces help with rhythm transitions
