Threshold Adaptation - August 12

Threshold Adaptation
Marathon pace control training
Total duration
50-55 min
Work pace
5:50-6:05/km
Approximate distance
12-14 km

Session Structure

10 min warm-up (6:45-7:00/km): Gradual activation, prepare body for work
6 min threshold block (5:50-6:05/km): Comfortable threshold pace, faster than marathon but sustainable
2 min active recovery (7:00-7:15/km): Easy jog to recover between blocks
Repeat 4 times: 4 x (6 min threshold + 2 min recovery)
8-10 min cool-down (6:50-7:10/km): Gradual return to easy pace

Key Points

  • Pace control: Focus on consistent effort rather than heart rate monitoring
  • Breathing rhythm: Maintain steady, controlled breathing throughout work blocks
  • Recovery discipline: Use recovery intervals to truly recover, don't rush
  • Marathon adaptation: This pace builds your threshold without overreaching
  • Body awareness: Notice posture and form during faster segments
